Our story

Cooked over real fire, every single plate.

Red Flame started with one charcoal grill and a simple rule: if it can't stand up to real heat, it doesn't go on the menu. Today that same grill still anchors the kitchen — every burger, steak and slice passes over open flame before it reaches your table.

We keep the ingredient list short and the sourcing local: fresh vegetables from Kano's markets, meat butchered daily, dough proofed in-house. It's a small menu, done properly.
